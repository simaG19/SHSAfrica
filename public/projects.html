What We Do

Our Active Programs

Each project is designed around our four strategic pillars, working in close collaboration with partner hospitals and government institutions.

01

Advanced Clinical Training Program

We deliver hands-on training in obstetric, neonatal, and surgical procedures tailored to the needs of each partner hospital. Programs include simulation-based emergency drills, Continuing Medical Education (CME) accredited courses, and leadership training for department heads.

Emergency obstetric & neonatal simulation drills
CME-accredited workshops and mentorship
Quality improvement and leadership training
02

Health Infrastructure & Equipment

We procure, install, and integrate essential medical equipment in partner hospitals to strengthen NICU, labor ward, and postnatal care capabilities. Our approach includes biomedical engineering support and digital health system integration for patient tracking.

NICU and labor ward equipment upgrades
Biomedical engineering & maintenance support
Digital health system integration
03

Research & Data Systems Strengthening

We support partner hospitals in establishing clinical research units, training staff in research methodology and ethics, and strengthening Health Management Information Systems (HMIS). Our goal is to build a culture of evidence-based practice and data-driven decision making.

Clinical research units in partner hospitals
Research methodology & ethics training
HMIS data systems strengthening
04

Family Planning Integration

We integrate family planning counseling and services into antenatal, delivery, and postnatal care pathways. Our programs include LARC training for providers, adolescent-friendly reproductive health services, and community mobilization for demand creation.

Integrated FP counseling in MNCH services
Long-acting contraceptive (LARC) training
Adolescent reproductive health services

Who We Are

Committed to Sustainable Medical Excellence in Africa

SHS Africa partners with tertiary hospitals across Ethiopia to strengthen healthcare systems from the ground up. We believe that every community deserves access to world-class medical care.

Through equipment integration, medical training, and long-term institutional partnerships, we are building healthcare infrastructure that lasts. Our approach prioritizes local ownership and knowledge transfer to ensure sustainable impact.

What We Do

Our Core Pillars of Impact

We focus on three interconnected areas to create lasting healthcare transformation across the continent.

Medical Equipment

Sourcing, delivering, and integrating critical medical equipment in partner hospitals to improve diagnostic and treatment capabilities.

Professional Training

Hands-on surgical and clinical training programs that empower local healthcare workers with world-class skills and knowledge.

Strategic Partnerships

Building lasting relationships between international medical institutions and African hospitals for ongoing mentorship and support.
